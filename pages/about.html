About Us

KindSteps is an initiative dedicated to supporting and rescuing mentally challenged children found on the streets.

Our team works closely with local organizations and volunteers to provide immediate aid and safe shelter to those in need.

We believe in empathy, action, and building bridges between affected children and helpful communities.

Whether you want to help, donate, or spread awareness, your involvement empowers vulnerable lives and creates positive change.
